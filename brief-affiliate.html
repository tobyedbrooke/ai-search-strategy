Affiliate & Editorial Team Brief

Fix how AI describes Trade Republic

AI systems build their answers from the sources that already rank well — comparison sites, editorial reviews, and financial media. The affiliate and editorial team has direct access to the people who write those pages. These are our highest-leverage external actions.

Why comparison and editorial sites matter for AI. When someone asks ChatGPT or Perplexity "which broker should I use in Germany?", the AI retrieves the top-ranking pages — Finanztip, Brokervergleich.de, Finanzfluss — and synthesises its answer from them. It does not visit traderepublic.com. Whatever those sites say about TR is what AI says about TR. Wrong fee, outdated rating, missing product — AI quotes it at scale, to every person who asks.

Priority 1 — Editorial (Finanztip, Stiftung Warentest, Handelsblatt)
Build the service improvement briefing document
Short term

Editorial sites need verifiable evidence, not claims. "We've improved" achieves nothing. A document with specific, auditable facts — agent headcount, support hours, CSAT scores, complaint trend — gives editors something they can cite in their review.

Briefing document should include

  • Current support agent headcount (1,000+) and availability (24/7)
  • Current CSAT score and trend (last 6 months)
  • Average first-response time (in-app chat and email)
  • vzbv complaint volume — absolute number and YoY trend
  • Languages supported
  • Channels available: in-app chat, phone, email (confirm which)

This document is the basis for all editorial outreach — build it once, use it across all sites.

Brief Finanztip and request a formal customer service score review
Short term
The specific problem Finanztip rates TR's customer service at 1 out of 7 — the lowest of all ten brokers they recommend. This is not an overall score; it is specifically the customer service category. AI cites this rating directly on every CS query. TR has since invested significantly in support (24/7, 1,000 agents) but Finanztip has not updated their rating.

Action: Send the briefing document to Finanztip's broker editorial team. Request a formal review meeting. Ask what criteria would need to be met for the score to move. Target: score updated within their next editorial cycle.

Note: Finanztip is editorially independent and will not change a score on request alone — the evidence must be genuinely compelling. Focus the conversation on their methodology: what they measured, when, and whether they'd re-test.

Submit evidence to Stiftung Warentest before their next broker test
Medium term

Stiftung Warentest conducts periodic broker tests that AI indexes directly. Their last test noted TR as leading in consumer complaints to the vzbv. Proactively submitting evidence before the next test influences the narrative rather than reacting to a published result.

Action: Identify their next scheduled broker test date. Submit the briefing document 4–6 weeks in advance. Request that the complaint narrative be reviewed before publication. Ask whether they accept supplementary data during their testing period.

Send a one-page product brief on every significant TR change going forward
Long term — standing process

AI quotes stale editorial data for months after a product change because editorial sites don't update proactively. Building a standing process — triggered by every rate, fee, or product change — keeps AI's picture of TR current.

Action: Every significant product change at TR triggers a one-page brief to the editorial contact list within one week. Assign one owner in the affiliate team. This process is the long-term infrastructure; the contact list built in the outreach phase is what makes it work.

Priority 2 — Comparison Sites (Brokervergleich.de, Finanzfluss, Vergleich.de, top-10)
Audit TR's data on all major comparison sites — log every error and gap
Short term

Wrong or stale data on comparison sites gets quoted by AI at scale. An outdated interest rate, a missing product, an incorrect fee — AI cites it with confidence to every person who asks. The audit comes first.

Action: Check TR's listing on the top 10 comparison sites. For each, log: incorrect fees, missing features, stale rates, absent products (e.g. crypto, fixed income, gold). Build a master error log — this becomes the basis for the correction pack.

Sites to cover: Brokervergleich.de, Finanzfluss, Vergleich.de, top10 broker ranking, BrokerDeal, Finanzcheck, Biallo, Verivox (broker section), Tarifcheck, Stiftung Warentest comparison tool.

Send an updated data pack to every major comparison site
Short term

Sites rarely update proactively — they rely on being contacted. A single standardised data pack corrects errors across all sites faster than chasing each individually.

Data pack should cover

  • Stock / ETF trade fee: €1 per order
  • ETF savings plan fee: €0
  • Interest on cash: current rate (update on every rate change)
  • Crypto available: yes — list assets
  • Fixed income / bonds: yes
  • Gold: yes
  • Account types: individual, joint (confirm), ISA equivalent
  • Markets: 17 European countries (list them)
  • Fractional shares: yes, from €1
  • Visa card: yes, included

Action: Send to all 10 sites. Follow up within one week to confirm corrections are live. A change on Brokervergleich.de alone affects thousands of AI responses weekly.

Review TR's affiliate rates vs competitors
Medium term

AI does not distinguish commercial ranking from editorial ranking. Comparison sites that earn more commission from a competitor rank that competitor higher — and AI cites those tables directly. Affiliate rates are one of the most direct levers for improving AI recommendation position.

Action: Obtain TR's current CPA and revenue-share rates for the top 5 comparison sites. Benchmark against Scalable Capital, XTB, and Trading212. Where TR's rate is meaningfully below market, assess whether raising it is cost-effective relative to the customer acquisition value of a higher AI ranking.

Expected outcome Finanztip updates its customer service score from 1/7. Comparison sites reflect current TR fees, rates, and products. AI answers about TR's fees, products, and service quality start citing accurate, current data from these sources rather than stale snapshots.
